Title:  Auden Quote

Link:   https://portablewisdom.org/quotes/the-only-sensible-procedure-for-a-critic.html

Attribution: -- W. H. Auden, From "The Dyer's Hand and Other Essays"

Teaser: The only sensible procedure for a critic is to keep silent about works which he believes to be bad, while at the same time vigorously campaigning for those which he believes to be good, especially if they are being neglected or underestimated by the public. 

Body:

The only sensible procedure for a critic is to keep silent about works which he believes to be bad, while at the same time vigorously campaigning for those which he believes to be good, especially if they are being neglected or underestimated by the public.
